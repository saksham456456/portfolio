# Timeline

## Recent focus
Recently I have been focused on AI-assisted interfaces, retrieval pipelines for small knowledge bases, and building user experiences that feel trustworthy instead of overconfident.

## Working style
I tend to work iteratively: define the smallest useful version, ship it, learn from the result, and tighten the experience over time.
